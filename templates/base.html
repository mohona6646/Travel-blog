Hi there, I'm Mohona



Hey there, my name is Mohona and as you can see from this website, I LOVE traveling. It is definitely one of my passions. I love learning about different cultures and get a taste of what it is like to be living there. I believe there is beauty in everything and traveling has taught me to have a greater appreciation of what Mother Nature has to offer to us. I hope this blog will grow your interest for traveling and will prepare you for your next destination, to wherever it is. Feel free to add pictures you have of the countries mentioned in this blog. This way, people can benefit from your tips and ideas too!

Before we jump right into the fun part, I would like to introduce myself. So far, you know my name and my love for travelling. I am 20 years old and live in Montreal, Cananda. I am currently studying at Concordia University in the program of Software engineering. I started travelling at a very young age with my family. I had this opportunity to experience the exhilaration one goes through when boarding a plane and wondering what the place will look like throughout the entire duration of the flight at the very age of 5. My very first journey was to Italy. The only thing I retained from my visit is the hazelnut gelato. Ever since, my love for traveling keeps growing!

Now that you know a little about me and that we both know we share the same passion for traveling, we can jump into the most awaited part of this blog, where we share our favourite moments and inspire others to do so! I hope this blog helps you guys to get started on your own journey and find the peace along the way. And remember, jobs fill your pocket. Adventures fill your soul.
